Roshan Khata — Privacy Policy

Har Hisaab Roshan · By Innovation-313

Last updated: 15 July 2026

The short version: Roshan Khata keeps your business records on your own phone. We do not run a server, we do not collect your ledger, and we never sell or share your data. Cloud backup, if you turn it on, goes to your own Google Drive — not to us.

1. Who we are

Roshan Khata ("the app") is a digital ledger (bahi-khata) for small businesses, built by Innovation-313. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Contact: innovation313.support@gmail.com

2. What data the app stores

The app stores the business records you enter — customer names, phone numbers, transactions, balances, cheques, payment plans, and bills. All of this is kept locally on your device. It is not sent to us, and we have no access to it.

3. Google Drive backup (optional)

If you choose to connect Google Drive, the app can back up your records so you can recover them if you change or lose your phone. Please note:

  • The backup goes to your own Google Drive, using your Google account. It does not come to us.
  • The backup is stored in the app's own private folder on your Drive (appDataFolder). The app cannot see or touch any of your other Drive files, documents, or photos.
  • The app requests only one Drive permission — access to its own app-data folder — and nothing more.
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from within the app.
  • We do not read, collect, store, or share the contents of your backup. It stays in your Drive, under your control.

4. File backup

The app can also save a backup file to your phone's Downloads, which you can then share (for example over WhatsApp) or keep yourself. This file stays on your device and wherever you choose to send it. We are not involved.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or partners.
  • We do not use your ledger for analytics or profiling.
  • We do not track your location.

6. Permissions the app uses

  • Internet — only to upload/download your backup to your own Google Drive, when you turn cloud backup on.
  • Google Drive (app-data folder only) — to store your backup in your own Drive, as described above.
  • Fingerprint / device lock — only to lock the app on your device, if you enable App Lock. This never leaves your phone.

7. Your rights

  • You can delete any record, or all your data, from within the app.
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time.
  • You can delete the backup file from your Drive or your phone yourself.

8. Children

The app is intended for business owners and is not directed at children.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For any question about your privacy or this policy, contact us at innovation313.support@gmail.com.
